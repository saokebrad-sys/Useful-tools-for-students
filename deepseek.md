DeepSeek is an advanced artificial intelligence model designed to process natural language and generate human-like responses,
making it a powerful tool for learning and information retrieval. Unlike traditional search engines that simply provide links,
DeepSeek offers direct, context-aware answers, explanations, and summaries, which helps learners quickly grasp complex topics without
sifting through multiple sources. It supports education by breaking down difficult concepts, generating practice questions, 
offering step-by-step problem-solving guidance, and adapting explanations to different knowledge levels.
As a source of information, DeepSeek draws from a vast range of publicly available data, but it also encourages critical thinking by
prompting users to verify facts from authoritative references when needed.
