Canva is a free online design tool that lets anyone make pro-looking graphics without design skills. It works in your browser or phone app, and it’s all drag
-to-down
 

 ways students actually use it

1. Presentations that don't look boring
- Ditch Google Slides. Pick an animated template, drop in your points, add charts/QR codes
- Export as PPT, PDF, or present directly from Canva with click animations

2. Infographics for reports
- Turn a wall of text into a visual timeline, comparison chart, or process diagram
- Teachers love these for science, history, business assignments

3. Posters + flyers
- Club events, campaigns, school projects. Print-ready sizes built in
- No more MS Word + clip art from 2003
